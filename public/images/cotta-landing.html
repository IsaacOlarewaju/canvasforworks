The availability of media representing the black community does not currently meet its demand.

There’s power in numbers. Uniting the creative talent that exists across the black community globally, the goal is to economically empower creators directly through image licensing and also by leveraged exposure and networking opportunities.

What Cotta will achieve can ultimately be embodied by three pillars:

Empowering photographers by curating the platform which will enhance their exposure, at the same time as inviting them into a rich network of peers, audiences, clients, and partners.

Delivering high resolution images, providing quality resources which will benefit an ecosystem of hobbyists, designers, brands & businesses, and media agencies of all scales.

Sharing authentic culture, supporting photographers in freely sharing their essence, which gifts creators with the power of the narratives. Allowing stories to be told by those who truly live them, all across the world.

In a generation that’s fuelled by visual media, creators can take control of our own narratives, share stories from our perspectives and immortalise our lived experiences into history. We are all creators.
